Image Stack Interactions

1. Click-Through Carousel
Click anywhere to cycle through images
2. Hover Zones
Hover left/right to navigate
3. Progress Bar Scrubber
Click on progress bar to jump to position
4. Keyboard Navigation
Click to focus, then use arrow keys or 1,2,3
5. Auto-Rotate with Pause
Auto-cycles, hover to pause
6. Corner Peek
Hover edges to peek at next/previous
7. Dot Navigation
Click dots to jump to specific image
8. Swipe Gestures
Drag left/right to swipe between images
9. Scroll-Triggered
Mouse wheel over image to navigate
10. Double-Click Zoom
Single click to navigate, double-click for fullscreen
